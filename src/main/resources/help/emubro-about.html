About emuBro

"emuBro is a game launcher that associates your games with your emulators such as snes9x, epsxe, pcsx2, dolphin or whatever you like."

What does it means? I don't understand a word!

Quite simply, emuBro lists your games and let you organize, manage and of course launch them.

Maybe you already use other launchers like Steam, Origin, Battle.net or something like that to play your PC games.
emuBro is just that, a game launcher. But it was mainly built to run retro games.

For example:
Games, such as Crash Bandicoot for the Sony PlayStation can be played very well on a computer with Windows, Linux or Mac on it.

You are kidding me, right?!"

No its true! This is called emulation. And what do you think you need to emulate something? Just right, an emulator!
